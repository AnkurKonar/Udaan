The club endeavours to hone their creative talents and give a nurturing platform to them by organising regular sessions. What are the events
undertaken by Udaan?
Events
Gupshup
Date : 20th March, 2018

A fun casual event was organized by Udaan with an aim to remove stage fright and introduce the students of IIIT Kalyani to the art of public speaking. Students came on the stage and discussed their love for literature. Interest shown by the audience in topics ranging from intricacies of a woman to social issues and from anime to Robert Frost's deep works was commendable.
Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat
Date : 21st March, 2018

The March edition of the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat event took the audience on a ride through the rich heritage of Rajasthan and the scenic beauty of Assam. Following two programs were organized by Udaan to celebrate these two beautiful states of India and at the same time to hone the literray skills of the students of IIIT Kalyani.

1. Group Discussion : Participants expressed their opinion on the Padmavat issue and the reaction of the native Rajasthanis on the same. In the process participants got an opportunity to work and improve on their communication skills and imbibe in themselves leadership ability. At the same time the audience learned a lot about the entire controversy surrounding Padmaavat.

2. WordMania : The heat generated due to the intense group discussion on the Padmaavat issue was cooled down by this fun game of words. In this open to all event words pertaining to the culture of Assam were given out to the audience and people were supposed to make as many words as they could from the word that was given to them. It was enjoyed by students and faculty members alike.
Le Tour De India
Date : 11th February, 2018

Le Tour De India was an engrossing and entertaining quiz competition organized by Udaan under the February edition of Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat. It took the students of IIIT Kalyani on an enlightening voyage through India covering on topics such as culture, governance, wildlife, tourism and industries.
IIITK-MUN
Date : 31st May,2018

Udaan and Semblance (The Dramatics Club) organised the first ever IIIT-K MUN on the topic "The Situation in the South-East due to the Israel-Palestine Conflict." The conference provided the students of IIIT Kalyani a platform to step into the shoes of a diplomat, meaningfully engage with the contemporary word issues, polish their public speaking and communication skills and at the same time provided them an opportunity to become more confident and articulate global citizens.
